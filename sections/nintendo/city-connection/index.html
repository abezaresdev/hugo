City Connection

Apr 25, 2024 · 1 min read
nintendo

City Connection is a platformer video game developed by Jaleco for the NES. Players control a car named Clarice as they drive through various cities and avoid obstacles. With its unique gameplay mechanics and colorful visuals, it provides an entertaining experience for players.

In City Connection, players must paint every section of the road while avoiding police cars and other hazards. The game’s simple yet addictive gameplay and catchy music have cemented its status as a classic NES title, appealing to gamers of all ages.
